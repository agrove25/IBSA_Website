About Us

Indonesian Bruins Student Association (IBSA) is a UCLA based student organization that promotes and celebrates Indonesian culture in the UCLA community. In addition, IBSA also promotes Indonesian art and culture in the broader UCLA community and aims to integrate others of various backgrounds that have a common interest in Indonesia and its culture.

As a result of these goals, IBSA attempts to serve as a reminder for Indonesian students of their native heritage and as encouragement for other UCLA students who are not familiar with Indonesia to increase their knowledge and interest in our culture. IBSA is a nonprofit registered organization under the Center for Student Programming at UCLA and is one of the many Southeast Asian groups at UCLA. IBSA also aims to be involved in cultural, social and community projects that promote a better understanding of the Indonesian culture.
